Whoami and what is this website.

XANDER "ALEXANDER" JOHN

Freelance Illustrator

PROFESSIONAL
Freelance artist working and developing illustrations and sketches for clients. Building off my formal BFA education at NMU I'm currently looking to continue my craft and bring my creative ideas, skill, and expertise to a game or design studio and am always looking for new and challenging commissions or contracts.

PERSONAL
I grew up in the forest, raised by squirrels and goblins. My first video game was Ogre Battle: March of the Black Queen so everything released since has been disappointing. The first book I read from my school library was a scholastic The Vikings culture, language, and exploration book that I took home with me, I still have it - I stole it.
